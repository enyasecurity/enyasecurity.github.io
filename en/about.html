About Enya Security

Enya Security helps small and medium-sized businesses and associations improve their cybersecurity through clear, educational, and non-intrusive audits.

About the founder

My name is Marina Bruno, an information systems engineer and founder of Enya Security.

For several years, I have supported organizations in analyzing information systems, resolving incidents, producing technical documentation, and improving business processes. This experience allowed me to develop a rigorous, methodical approach focused on finding concrete solutions.

Passionate about cybersecurity, I deepened my skills through specialized training, hands-on labs, and recognized certifications, including Ethical Hacking Essentials (EC-Council) and Pre-Security (TryHackMe).

I created Enya Security with a simple goal: make cybersecurity audits accessible to small and medium-sized businesses and associations through a clear, educational, and non-intrusive approach.

Audits are independent, confidential, and pragmatic. My goal is not only to identify vulnerabilities but above all to explain the risks they represent and to offer concrete recommendations tailored to the reality of each organization.
